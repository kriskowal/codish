to return a two dimensional collection where each [row] corresponds to the [col] of another 2 [dimension]al [collection].
related: zip
aka: unzip
inverse: transpose

<p><tt>transpose([[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6]]) eq [[1, 4], [2, 5], [3, 6]]</tt></p>
<p><tt>transpose([[1, 2, 3], [4, 5]]) eq [[1, 4], [2, 5]]</tt></p>
<p><tt>transpose([[1, 2], [4, 5, 6]]) eq [[1, 4], [2, 5]]</tt></p>
